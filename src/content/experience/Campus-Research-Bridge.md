Alongside my cofounder, I created Campus Research Bridge. Campus Research Bridge connected businesses and college students, so that businesses could get feedback from their target demographic, and so college kids could earn a little extra cash for their participation. 

Led in-person focus groups during the 2023-24 academic year, providing clients with in-depth reports based on data collected from student participants before, during, and after each session.

Helped clients develop a more nuanced understanding of their customer base. For example, contributed insights that helped Curtis Butler finalize pricing and packages for the career coaching business he launched shortly after the focus group.

Oversaw engineering of a full-stack web application built with Django, React, and MongoDB to conduct online panel discussions and surveys with participants in the research pool.

Participated with a board of advisors that mentored my knowledge of management, business intelligence, legal operations, market research, and software development.

(We also helped college students earn a little extra beer money)
